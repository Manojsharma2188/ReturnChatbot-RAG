RETURNS POLICY

1. RETURN WINDOW
You may return items within 30 days of delivery.

2. REFUND TIMELINE
Refunds are processed within 3 to 5 business days after we receive your return.

3. ELIGIBLE ITEMS
All items must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging with tags attached.

4. RETURN SHIPPING
Free return shipping on all orders within the continental United States.

5. EXCHANGES
Exchanges are processed within 2 business days after we receive your item.

6. DAMAGED ITEMS
If you receive a damaged item, please contact customer service within 48 hours.

7. INTERNATIONAL RETURNS
International returns may take 10 to 15 business days for refund processing.
